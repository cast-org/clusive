renewable

(adjective) Able to be replaced.
Renewable resources include air, plants, water, and animals, and other things that are replaceable within a person's lifetime.

(noun) a source of energy that is not depleted by use, such as water, wind, or solar power. production costs for the renewables are higher than for coal, oil, or gas
