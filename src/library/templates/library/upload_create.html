How to upload content

Content needs to be in EPUB format to be uploaded into Clusive. EPUB is a common format for electronic books.

Find EPUBs at Standard eBooks, Tar Heel Reader, or other sites.

Create EPUBs from Word documents with the free DAISY WordToEpub Converter (Windows only), or from open resources using OER Commons OpenAuthor.
